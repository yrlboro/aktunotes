# CF2 — Probabilitas & Statistika

Modul CF2 mencakup teori probabilitas, variabel acak, distribusi gabungan, dan statistika inferensial. Bobot ujian berkisar antara **30–40 soal pilihan ganda**.

> [!tip] Referensi Utama
> Hogg, Tanis & Zimmerman — *Probability and Statistical Inference* · Wackerly, Mendenhall & Scheaffer — *Mathematical Statistics*
